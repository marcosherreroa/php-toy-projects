Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 500g minced beef
  • 800g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, to serve

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and cook until softened.
  3. Add the minced beef, cooking until browned. Break up the meat with a wooden spoon as it cooks.
  4. Stir in the chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, and oregano. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Season the sauce with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti. Top with grated Parmesan cheese.
