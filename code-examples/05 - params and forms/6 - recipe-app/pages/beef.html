Beef Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil, divided
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the beef slices with soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of sesame oil, and cornstarch. Mix well and let it marinate for 15 minutes.
  2. Heat the remaining sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the marinated beef and cook for 2-3 minutes until browned. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the garlic, ginger, and onion. Stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the bell peppers and broccoli to the skillet. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Return the beef to the skillet and add the oyster sauce. Stir everything together and cook for another 1-2 minutes, ensuring the beef is fully cooked and everything is well-coated with the sauce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve the beef stir-fry hot over cooked rice.
